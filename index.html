About Helping Hands

Helping Hands is a community driven volunteer program that provides structure, resources, and event planning support to Members who wish to give back to their local community. Join us today, and you can work closely with our network of local leaders to plan, attend, or donate to various projects and events that seek to nurture a healthy community.

Our past projects include ocean clean-up events, community garden construction, and meal preparation for local food banks. As the Covid-19 pandemic has affected many people in our neighborhoods, our community-support efforts now include the procurment and delivery of personal protective equipment to those who need it most.

Sign up today, and help us build a thriving community for everyone!
